My story

I started PB&J Designs for two reasons:

  1. Developers deserve products with great UX, too. So many developer tools don’t invest in their UX because “developers don‘t care about good design.” I disagree.
  2. I wanted more time to spend with my daughter. I got burned out on the rat race of climbing the corporate ladder and wanted more control and autonomy over my time. Running my own solo design agency has given me the flexibility I desired.

If any of this resonates with you, reach out. I’m a former developer who’s been designing technical tools for over 10 years now, and building on the web for more than 20.

Services include UI/UX work, from research to polished UIs, workshops to train developers and PMs in UX methods, and UX Audits.
